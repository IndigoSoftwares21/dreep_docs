---
title: 'Cloudinary Migration'
description: 'Connect your Cloudinary account to easily import existing media assets into Dreep.'
---

Dreep supports direct integration with your existing Cloudinary account. By connecting Cloudinary, you can seamlessly browse and import your legacy images and videos directly into your Dreep folders without needing to manually download and re-upload them.

## Locating your Cloud Name

To connect your account, you will need your **Cloud name**. Note that this is not your username or account name, but the unique identifier for your media environment.

1. Log into your [Cloudinary Dashboard](https://cloudinary.com/console).
2. Look for the **Product Environment** section near the top of the dashboard.
3. Your **Cloud name** is displayed there alongside your Folder mode.

<img src="/images/cloudinary-cloud-name.png" alt="Cloudinary Dashboard showing the Cloud name" />

## Connecting the Integration

Once you have your Cloud name:

1. Open your Dreep project and navigate to the **Integrations** page.
2. Click **Connect** on the Cloudinary card.
3. Enter your Cloud name. You will be redirected to Cloudinary to authorize Dreep to access your account via OAuth.
4. After authorization, you will be redirected back to Dreep.

## Importing Assets

After successfully connecting, click **Import Assets** on the Cloudinary card. 

You can browse your entire Cloudinary media library, select multiple files, choose a destination folder in Dreep, and start the import. Dreep runs the transfer entirely in the background, so you can safely close the window while it progresses!
